AI/ML

AI (Artificial Intelligence) and ML (Machine Learning) are two closely related terms that refer to the use of technology and algorithms to enable computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. AI is a broad field that encompasses a range of technologies, including ML, natural language processing (NLP), computer vision, robotics, and more. AI is concerned with developing intelligent agents or systems that can reason, learn, and act autonomously. ML, on the other hand, is a specific technique within AI that involves training algorithms to learn from data. In ML, a computer program is trained on a set of data, and it uses that training to make predictions or decisions about new data. ML algorithms can be categorized into supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning.
